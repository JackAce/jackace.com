Privacy Policy Overview

Last Updated: 11 Jan, 2025

jackace.com ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website, jackace.com (the "Website"). By accessing or using the Website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from users, such as names, email addresses, or contact details. However, third-party services we use, such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics, may collect certain information about your visit to the Website.

Automatically Collected Information

The following data may be collected automatically:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs, and click behavior.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on your IP address.

This data is anonymized wherever possible and used for analytics and advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The following data may be collected automatically:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Website. Google Analytics may collect and process data, including:

The pages you visit and how long you spend on them. Your approximate location, browser, and device information.

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy and Google Analytics Terms. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on the Website. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your browsing behavior.

  • Personalized Ads: Based on your interests and previous interactions with websites.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: Based on the content of the page rather than your behavior.

For more information about how Google uses cookies and how to manage your ad preferences, visit Google’s Advertising Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable essential website functionality.
  • To analyze website performance and usage.
  • To deliver advertisements via Google AdSense.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

How we Manage Cookies

We use a third-party tool, Cookie-Script, to manage cookie consent on our website. Cookie-Script ensures that we comply with data protection laws, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

When you visit our website, Cookie-Script:

  • Detects and categorizes all cookies used on the site.
  • Displays a cookie consent banner to allow you to manage your preferences.
  • Stores your consent choice for future visits.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time using the [Cookie Settings] link available on our website.

Consent Logging

Your cookie consent preferences are logged by Cookie-Script and stored securely. This data includes:

  • The user's consent state (e.g., accepted, rejected).
  • A unique identifier tied to the consent.
  • Timestamp of the consent action.

These records are retained to comply with legal obligations and ensure accountability under applicable laws.

How to Opt-Out of Cookies

  • For Google Analytics: Use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-On.
  • For Google Ads: Visit the Ad Settings Page.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, anonymized and aggregated data may be shared with:

  • Google for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • Service providers assisting in website performance monitoring.
