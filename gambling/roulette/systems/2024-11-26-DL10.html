--- layout: roulette-system title: DL10 - Roulette System Review ---

D'Alembert with 10 numbers straight-up. Just pick any 10 numbers. Not sure why there was a blank spin.

When you win, pull back a unit until you only have 1 unit on each bet. When you lose, increase by $1 on each bet.

You just keep pressing until you can't bet anymore. This is like other D'Alembert variants. I've only shown the payouts for the first 3 tiers, but just multiply $26 by your unit bet to figure out how much you're going to win.
