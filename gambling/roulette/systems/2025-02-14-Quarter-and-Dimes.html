This is a goofy ass {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" %} system. You basically bet on two Dozens and juice the bets with two Street bets. If you hit the jackpot, then you YOLO the winnings on a bunch of Straight-Up bets.

What makes this system "goofy" is that I am pretty sure Alex just slapped a bunch of bets on the table after hitting those jackpot wins. I don't think there was any rhyme or reason for picking the numbers and the amounts that he bet. The only stipulation is that they all added up to the most recent win.

Since there's no real definition to those higher tier "house money" bets, there's no real way I can evaluate this system accurately. I can't tell you how successful you will be with a system like this because it all depends on the strict definition of those big bets.
