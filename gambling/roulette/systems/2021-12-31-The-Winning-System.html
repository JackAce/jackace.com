--- layout: roulette-system title: The Winning System - Roulette System Review ---

You start with the $50/$50 bet on two dozens.

When you hit on the first tier, you pull back the winning bet and try to win the other Dozen bet. If that bet loses, then start over.

If you lose on the first tier, then keep pressing the two bets, going from $100 to $200, to $300, to $500, to $700. When you win on the negative progression, then reset to tier 1.

This is a very risky system that gets out of control pretty quickly.
