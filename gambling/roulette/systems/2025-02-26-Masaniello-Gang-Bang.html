Betting goes according to this chart:

You start on the upper-left hand corner and you start with a blank spin. If it's red/odd, then go down if it's black/even, go right. If you're right/above the diagonal, then bet Black/Even. If you're left/below the diagonal, bet Red/Odd. Multiply the number by your unit bet size.

Don't play multi-leg systems. You're just adding multiple single points of failure to your system.
