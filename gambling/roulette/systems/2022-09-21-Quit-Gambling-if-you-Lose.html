Two bullets - $500, then 5x {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Martingale" %} to $2500 if you lose. Then cry if you lose your second bullet.

This is a super high risk system. Hitting one of 8 whacks two times in a row will happen a lot more than you think.

Probability of hitting two whacks in a row on 2 spins:

\begin{align} P_{2\,whacks} & = [\frac{8}{38}] ^2 \\ \\ & = 4.432\% \\ \end{align}
