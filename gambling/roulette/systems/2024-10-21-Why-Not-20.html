You bet High and 2nd and 3rd Dozens with $5 each. Both Dozens have to overlap with the Even Money bet.

When you win $10 on the first tier, you move to tier 2.

On the other tiers, you can press your winnings on the winning bets.

Alex and David were changing the system on the fly. It's hard to figure out which parts were part of the original system they ended up rating.

The original system called for betting Low + 2nd and 3rd Dozens. That combo results in betting on all 36 numbers, which is a no-no. I'm glad they changed the system.
