Negative Progression system. Just pick any 7 numbers and bet them straight-up.

Start with $1 per number, then $3, $5, $7, $13, $21, $35, $60, and $100.

I don't like betting so many numbers straight-up. I'd probably bet at least a double-street, or I might just bet two Corners instead (changing the base bet size).

I also think the progression is too long. I would only do 4 or 5 levels instead. 9 levels is way too much.

Alex just kept buying in for more trying to chase his losses.
