--- layout: roulette-system title: Skipped The Fib - Roulette System Review ---

A weird implementation of Fibonacci. You don't repeat the first one unit bet. It's a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" -%}, but there are rules for when you reset.

Both bets act together, so this isn't a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Multi-Leg" %} system.

When you're on a losing streak, you don't reset to step 1 until you've won 2x in a row.
