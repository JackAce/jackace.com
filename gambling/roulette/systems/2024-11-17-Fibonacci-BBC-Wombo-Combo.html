This is a ladder system where you Fibonacci on a loss, and you go into a complicated positive progression when you win. For the Fibonacci sequence, it's 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, etc. where you add the last two bets. The base unit is $25 for this, so the sequence is 25, 25, 50, 75, 125, 200, etc.

For the Fibonacci sequence, I'm not going to provide the system bet images. You should just recognize that you're going to win 2x your bet.

For the positive progression part, you bet $20 each on two overlapping Even money bets.
