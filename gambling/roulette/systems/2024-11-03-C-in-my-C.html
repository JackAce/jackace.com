A.K.A. "Color in my Column"

This is a Positive Progression system. When you win the small bet, press your winnings for the next tier. If you push, stay on the same level. {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=35 %} or push, stay on tier 2.

On every tier, you're betting 2 units. You either get whacked, you push, you win 1 unit, or you win 3 units.

If you hit a big jackpot, you can either reset to tier 1 or press the winnings.

I gave this system a {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="b" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false -%}. It would be an {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="a" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false %} if there were more opportunity to win big.
