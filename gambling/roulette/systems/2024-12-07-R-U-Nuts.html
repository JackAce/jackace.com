This is a slow-moving, low-limit Negative Progression. You start out with a $2 Split bet. After losing 5x, increase to $3. After every 5 losses, increaase to the next tier. Reset to tier 1 on a win.

This system isn't bad. I think I might just Martingale with every 5th bet instead of the slow uptick this system is doing.
