--- layout: roulette-system title: The Clencher - Roulette System Review ---

This is a somewhat complicated {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Ladder" %} system where you increase your bet by half a unit when you lose, but YOLO/Parlay your wins when you hit.

You start out with two $10 bets each on two Dozens or Columns. If you lose both bets, increase each bet by $5.

Whenever you hit, take all the money on the table and bet it all on a single Dozen or Column.
