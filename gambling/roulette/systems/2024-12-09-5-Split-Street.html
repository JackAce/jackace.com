Tier 1: Bet $1 each on five Streets, and $1 each on five Splits, not overlapping with the Street bets. If you lose, then D'Alembert by adding another $1 on all ten bets. When you hit a Street, then remove $1 from every bet (until there's just $1 left). When you hit a Split bet, always reset to tier 1.

Walk when you're up $100 or more.

This is a good low level system.
