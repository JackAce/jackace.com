Ridiculous system where you bet one number, bet 2, then 3...all the way up to 12 (and fill up a dozen block), and then {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Martingale" %} that dozen until you win.

I'm only providing a graphic for the first tier of betting, where you win $175. For every nickel you add, you lose $5 of that win. So if you bet $15, the win is only $165.
