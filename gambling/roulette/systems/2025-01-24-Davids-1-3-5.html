--- layout: roulette-system title: David's 1-3-5 - Roulette System Review ---

This is a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" -%}. When you win, go on to the next tier.

You basically start with a Dozen/Column, bet. Parlay that win to an even money bet. When that wins, peel off the initial bet and put the rest back on a Dozen/Column.

If you go through that cycle, you can repeat with a two unit initial bet. This continues through the 3 step process, but they never passed the 5th tier, so I can't be 100% sure.

I like this system. It's a good low limit grinder. Seems like there are lots of opportunities for profit taking with bets on high percentage winning bets (Even Money and 2:1s). This is a great low limit system.
