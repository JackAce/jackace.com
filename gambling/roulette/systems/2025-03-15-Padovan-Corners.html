This is the {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Negative Progression" %} that I reviewed several weeks ago. It is a modification of the Padovan Streets system, but with a larger target.

Bet $10 spread across two non-overlapping Corner bets. Increase on a loss according to the Padovan Sequence, which is 1, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 16, 21, 28, 37...

When you win, reset to tier 1.
