--- layout: roulette-system title: UGF - Roulette System Review ---

UGF stands for "Ultimate Gambler's Fallacy", so you're supposed to bet against a streak. You do that by betting the losing Even Money bets from the last number that hit.

Bet the three opposite Even Money bets of the number that just hit. Sit out after a {% include roulette-spot.html number="0" %} or {% include roulette-spot.html number="00" %} and wait for a field number.

It's really a waste of time to try to bet the opposite number. But it's not really harmful. But...ok. You're basically just betting 3 even money bets.
