This system is a multi-leg version of {% include roulette-system-link.html reviewUrl="2023-12-17-36DD" displayName="D'Alembert" -%}.

Make 4 bets: 3 even money bets and 1 column bet. $5 each bet. You will D'Alembert each bet independently, so increase each bet by one $5 when you lose. Decrease each bet by one $5 unit when you win. Each bet grows or shrinks independently as it hits or misses.

I would avoid betting dozens with this system, unless you pick the 2nd dozen, which intersects the High/Low bets with 6 numbers.

I have problems with multi-leg systems, especially negative progression multi-leg systems. It just seems like you're greatly increasing your chances of going broke by having 4 independent single points of failure.

Mostly because this is a Multi-Leg system I've given it a {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="d" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false -%}.

Because of the way that Alex bet this system, he ended up betting on all 36 numbers.
