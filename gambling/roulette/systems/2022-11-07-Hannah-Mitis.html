This is an absolute garbage system where you're betting on 36 numbers at a time.

Bet $10 on Red.

When you lose, double the losing bet and bet $10 on the opposite color.

So if you spin Black, then bet $10 on Black and $20 on Red.

Most of the time, you're going to end up with $10 on one color and some power of 2 times $10 on the other color.
