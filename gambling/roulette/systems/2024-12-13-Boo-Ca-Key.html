This system was advertised as a "Thruple's System", which means that a team of 3 people can play this strategy.

This is a positive progression system where you move to the next tier when you win.

We're only tracking Alex's bets.

Just ignore the "couple's system" aspect of this system. Just play this as a one-player system. If you play this as a couple's system or even a "thruple's system", you might end up betting on all 36 numbers at a time.

As a single-player system, this isn't bad. I definitely wouldn't use a $30 base bet. I'd either go with $25 or $50. And for the 3rd tier, I'd just put it all on a Dozen or a Column. They payout is going to be the same.
