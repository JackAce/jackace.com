This is a slow progresing {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" %} system.

After winning 3x in a row, go to the next step.

For the 3rd tier, you can either bet $90 on an Even Money bet, or you can bet 9 Splits at $10 each. Same Same.

This system has a $160 starting bet, which is HUGE, but then it drops down after the first tier...Which makes it NOT a good comp system. You can't play this on a single zero wheel, so It really hurts the rating for this system.
