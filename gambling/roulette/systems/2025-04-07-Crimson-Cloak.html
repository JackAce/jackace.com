--- layout: roulette-system title: Crimson Cloak - Roulette System Review ---

This is a goofy system where you "pick a color", and bet $110 on it. Then you pick 11 numbers of the opposite color and bet $10 on each. You push when you hit the color, you win {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=140 %} when you hit the straight-up numbers.

This is a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Flat Bet" %} system, although you have the option of switching between betting on Red and betting on Black. As for betting the "opposite" color, you can also bet on {% include roulette-spot.html number="0" %} or {% include roulette-spot.html number="00" %} as well. That's what Alex did with his Black bet configuration.

I'm not a fan of pushing. That is going to affect this system's ratings negatively.
