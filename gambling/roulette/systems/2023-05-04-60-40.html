--- layout: roulette-system title: 60/40 - Roulette System Review ---

You're supposed to bet 2 dozens that hit(?) after 2 blank spins (no bet). And then keep betting those dozens...

After betting $10 on each of the 2 dozens, you jump up to $60 each. After that you keep adding $60 per bet when you lose. For some reason, the system author called this 6x Martingale, which is not accurate.

If you win, then you step down to the previous betting tier.

Either the system author doesn't know how to properly describe their system or it was poorly executed by the CEG team. "6x Martingale" does NOT mean you add 6 units. And you don't even add 6 units when going from $10 to $60 on each bet.

This system doesn't really make any sense to me.
