This is also known as "All the Martingale" and "Martingale Gangbang"

Pick 3 overlapaping even money bets and bet 1 unit each. Pick one dozen and one column and bet 1 unit each. Martingale when you lose.

I gave this system a Durian because it's too complicated to keep track of each bet and you only add to the risk of losing everything by martingaling all the bets independently.

The typical Martingale system has three characteristics:

  • You win 1 unit when you win
  • It's easy to bet
  • You have a low probability of losing everything

This system has the following characteristics

  • You win up to 7 units when you win
  • It's NOT easy to bet
  • You have a MUCH HIGHER probability of losing everything

The negatives far outweigh the positives for me.
