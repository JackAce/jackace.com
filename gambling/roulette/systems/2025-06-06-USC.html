As a UCLA Bruin, eff this system. Literally. That's probably why Alex and CEG gave this an {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="F" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false -%}.

USC is supposed to stand for "Ultimate Couple's System"...but that don't work, does it?

I wouldn't give this system such a low score. It's not TERRIBLE, but again with the PUSHES!! No more pushes, please!

And things go from bad to worse if you really do play this as a "couple's system", as the system author suggests. The pushes will explode wildly if you do this.

This system doesn't do a good job of consolidating wins, and there are too many pushes. There's a narrow opportunity to win a small amount.
