--- layout: roulette-system title: Hit it and Split it - Roulette System Review ---

After hitting the 1st tier, take the winnings and bet any 10 Splits with $5 each. Make sure they don't overlap. If the 2nd tier hits, split the winnings and bet two Dozens of $45 each. If the 3rd tier hits, bet $10 each on 13 Splits. If that tier hits, bet $10 on 18 numbers Straight-up.
