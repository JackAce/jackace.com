A variant of Triple Entry Max Climax.

This is also a Spectrum Progression system, where the next betting tier might be one of several options, depending on the size of the win or loss. The first bet has 4 possible outcomes: {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=-30 -%}, {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=-10 -%}, {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=10 -%}, and {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=30 -%}.

This system is kinda all over the place. There are just too many ways your last win can turn into a bet that you've never bet before. Sometimes you split it back into 3 bets, sometimes you bet two dozens. Sometimes you bet a single dozen.

I don't really understand this system, so I'm giving it an {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="f" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false -%}.
