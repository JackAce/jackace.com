This is a "couple's system", but I'm going to combine the two bets into one.

Of course, by doing this they end up betting on all 38 numbers on the wheel.

{% include roulette-bet-nullification.html %}

But anyway, each person is responsible for two bets. Player 1 is responsible for the 1st and 2nd column. Player 2 is responsible for the the 3rd column and the {% include roulette-spot.html number="0" -%}/ {%- include roulette-spot.html number="00" %} Split. When each player loses both their bets, they press the bets by 1 $5 unit. When a player wins, they remove $5 from each of their bets until they only have $5 left on each bet. This is just D'Alembert.

You're also supposed to swap? Like when someone who is deep in the hole wins, they're supposed to change the bets they're responsible for? I dunno. This system is just a hot mess.
