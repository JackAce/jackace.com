Positive Progression system. Go to the next tier when you win. Reset to tier 1 when you lose.

This system is similar to Hopscotch, where your wins keep getting pressed as you inch forward in bet size. But I'm really not a fan of this one. The bets don't seem to continually grow in winnings. Sometimes they get larger, sometimes they get smaller. I don't like that.

I gave this system a {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="b" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false -%}, but it would probably be an {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="a" showRatingLetter=true showRatingName=false %} if the payouts weren't so low. I also don't like the continual press. I think it'e better to just go for larger hits with a smaller number of spots covered.
