"All Tall Small" - Roulette version.

When you win, increase your bet by 1 unit ($5). When you lose, increase your bet by 2 units ($10). Reset when you're up {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=25 %} or more.

Since this is a complex system, there is a large spectrum of bet amounts that you might make. These are all even money bets, so it doesn't make sense to create images for each of them.

Alex did a blank spin for spin number 0. High landed, so he bet High ({%- include roulette-spot.html number="19" -%}-{%- include roulette-spot.html number="36" -%}).

This is a goofy system where you're just increasing your bets no matter what. This means that your initial bets will usually just become negligable and everything is going to hinge on your biggest bets.
