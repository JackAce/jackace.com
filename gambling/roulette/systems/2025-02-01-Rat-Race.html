This is a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" %} system where you bet 4 Corners, and 2 Splits across 2 Dozens. When you win, press the Corner bets. Leave the Split bets the same.

That was the system as originally planned. Alex changed it up later where the 2nd tier only presses up $10 of the {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=15 %} win instead of doing a $20 power press. I think you only do this on the 2nd tier. You press $10 for tiers 2 and 3, but then press $20 on the rest.

Author suggests walking away when you're up $100, but it can be $200 or even $400. Alex more than doubled up with this.

Not a bad system. It has good jackpot potential.
