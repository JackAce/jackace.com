Positive Progression system. On the second tier, revert to the first tier if you hit the Double Street or if you lose. Don't change your bet if you win $20.

When you choose which Double Street to bet on, make sure it has 4 of your color in it, not 3 of each.

This is CEG's third iteration of this system, just tweaking the Double Street you're betting on based on how many of your color is in it.
