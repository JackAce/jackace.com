--- layout: roulette-system title: Line 'em up to get 'em up - Roulette System Review ---

Positive progression system that starts off like Hopscotch.

If you win 75 on the 3th tier, stay at the same $75 bet. If you hit the jackpot on tier 3, go to tier 4.

If you hit on the 4th tier, keep betting that same bet until you get whacked. Walk whenever you feel like it.
