This is a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Multi-Leg" %} system that uses a Ladder. There's a positive progression and a negative progression.

When you win, press $10 on the winning bet until it loses. When a winning streak is broken by a loss, then reset to the single unit bet.

When a bet loses, you're going to wait until it loses 3x. Then you go $15 then $20. The sequence is $10, $10, $10, $15, $20. When the losing streak is broken with a win, then reset to the single unit bet.
