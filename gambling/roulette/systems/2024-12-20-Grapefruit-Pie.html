This is a ladder system that starts out with {% include roulette-system-link.html reviewUrl="2024-02-08-Triple-Entry-Max-Climax" -%}, and then goes into {% include roulette-system-link.html reviewUrl="2024-10-14-Nolley-Molley" -%}.

Tier 1: Bet $15 each on 3 even money bets (not both sides of the same coin).

Result 1: Win 0/3 ({%- include win-loss-amount.html amount=-45 -%}): Stay on tier 1

Result 2: Win 1/3 ({%- include win-loss-amount.html amount=-15 -%}): Go to tier 2 (2x$15 Dozen bets).

Result 3: Win 2/3 ({%- include win-loss-amount.html amount=15 -%}): Go to tier 3 (2x$30 Dozen bets).

Result 4: Win 3/3 ({%- include win-loss-amount.html amount=45 -%}): Go to tier 4 (2x$45 Dozen bets).

When you win on a two Dozen bet (where the bets are equal), then you press with a Nolley bet on the dozens. So if you bet 2x$15 and you win {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=15 -%}, then you bet $15 and $30 on two dozens on the next bet.

When you win the big bet on the uneven double dozen bet, then press $5 on the small bet and bet two of them. So if you win {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=45 %} on the $30 + $15 bet, then you'd bet two dozens of $20 each.
