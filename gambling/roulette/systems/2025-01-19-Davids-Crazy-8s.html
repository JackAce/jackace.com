This system is a {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Positive Progression" %} that is similar to Paroli, except you're betting on a 2:1 bet instead of an even money bet.

Start out with a $10 bet on a dozen or column. When you win, increase your bet to $20 and move it to the next dozen/column. When that hits, move again to the remaining dozen/column. Win or lose on the 3rd tier, reset to tier 1.

This wouldn't be a bad system, but you're not going to win the dozens bet enough to go far with this. The double your bet strategy works best with even money bets.
