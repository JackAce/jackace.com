This is a Positive Progression system. After winning the first tier, you will bet all 3 OPPOSITE even money bets. So if you hit {% include roulette-spot.html number="21" %} (Red, Odd, High), then you would bet on Black, Even, Low.

After that you want to hit ALL THREEE bets then reset to step 1. If you only win 2/3, then you take your 4 units and bet 2 units each on two dozens.

If you only win 1/3, you take your 2 units and go back to step 1.
