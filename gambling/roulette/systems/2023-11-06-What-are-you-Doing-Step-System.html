Negative Progression

Start with either a corner or a Double Street bet of $25. You keep increasing your coverage of the felt by one unit when you lose. When you cover most of the felt (5 double streets or 6 corners), then start to {% include roulette-system-type.html type="Martingale" %} your bets until you've recovered.

Apparently, this system is supposed to be played as a team. "Team" systems are always worthless.

Alex played the Double Street version, David played the Corner bet version.

I gave this system a {% include ceg-system-rating.html rating="d" showRatingLetter=true -%}, because it's really not well thought out. It just seems like you're going to keep increasing your bets until you run into a brick wall. There's really very little chance of escaping failure.
