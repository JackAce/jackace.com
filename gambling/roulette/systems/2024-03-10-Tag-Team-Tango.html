As far as "2 player systems" go, you should NEVER bet on 36 or more numbers at a time between the two players.

Here you're betting $25 each on both Red and Black.

You're also betting $25 on each of the 3 dozens ($50 on the 2nd dozen).

All these bets should be eliminated, since each bet pays the others off when they win, but they ALL lose when 0/00 hit.

If you're playing as a "team", then it doesn't make sense to make these bets.

Remove these bets, and you've reduced the $170 in bets to just $45. Your expected loss per spin goes down from -$8.94 to -$2.37, saving over $6 in EV per spin.
